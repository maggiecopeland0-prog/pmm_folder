## Required Terms

| Always say | Never say | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| customer | client | Company standard |
| overview | summary (for AI outputs) | Distinguishes AI output from human summaries |
| dashboard, search, reports | UI | Name the specific surface  -  "UI" is vague |
| platform | backend, system | Meaningful to non-technical audiences |
